"Tears on Paper, Echoes of Sorrow"

Have you ever felt like you've hit rock bottom in life? The kind of moment where everything seems dark and hopeless. It's a difficult place to be in, no doubt. But did you know that writing sonnets can be a great way to express those emotions in words? Our group wrote our own sonnets about various similar experiences, and our goal is to capture the different forms of sadness one feels during those tough times.
By reflecting on various experiences and putting them into words, one is able to release repressed emotions and find a sense of calm and peace. It's like turning pain into something beautiful.
Expressing emotions through sonnets is a creative way to deal with sadness and move towards healing. The sonnets capture the different forms of sadness and help in reflecting on past events that trouble us. Writing about sad experiences allows for the release of repressed emotions in a calm and meditative way, leading to gradual recovery and moving on.
So, if you're going through a tough time, remember that you have the power to overcome it. Take some time to write down your feelings in a sonnet. This might even become a new, helpful hobby to help you get through rough times without the unnecessary drama.

By: Tristan Catap

A song awaken, its name Incomplete
My sad emotions on this vocal swell

A deep desire rises, passionate in scope,
of this awful tune, in the shadows pressed
A plea that echoes through love's passionate slope
Fill my spaces with hope, heartfully expressed

"My Broken Self in a Perfect World"

By: Joshua Chua

In a world without fault, what is my purpose as someone flawed?
Am I nothing but a hindrance while my gentle soul rotted away?
As if all my past mistakes have led me astray
And shattered my wings as if it's getting clawed.
The ideal me, now small fragments of feelings thats sawed
I had a good life, yet my hands chased hard dreams and didnt stay.
If only I knew my chances of achieving then are quite gray.
And now, I must endure everything I started, with hope outlawed.
But in these moments of gloom, I can also think of improvements.
I may have lots of flaws but I'm not full of them.
I can still save myself from becoming forever gloomy.
My whole life I've kept myself in a cage of concealments,
But when I accept my mistakes and who I am
I can escape my cage and find my perfect wings to fly freely.

"Heaven's Darling"

By: Andrea Martinez

Sealed the final chapter of life’s beautiful memories with you
Recollections of a life’s beloved years
Father had always told me to hold back from my sorrowful tears
Yet Mother, each blink brings a tear, as sorrow flows through
Shattered to pieces, I lay here weeping in sorrow, yet I knew
A life lived with passion, is a life that endears
A broken heart was once a heart that felt love through the years
I wish to see the world through your eyes, from your rose-colored view
A darling from the heavens, in the shape of my mother
When I was at my lowest, you raised me high
You saw the individual who I grew to be, with a pure heart and mind
Spread your wings and take flight, as I know you like no other
“Hallelujah”, He said as He took you back to His kingdom in the sky
“You’ve arrived”

"Emptiness Without Them"

By: Joshua Chua

My feelings are bleak, with thoughts never-ending.
It's just not the same without them with me.
A day of joy becomes one of lamenting,
The death of memories we all share with glee.
Replaying old thoughts so I can rewind,
to times I didn't despise myself so often.
The only thing keeping me together was them by my side.
Oh, what can I do to stop being so solemn?
And how do I express my sorrow,
when I don't let myself speak and instead sink?
Maybe saying what our stuttering voices felt, concisely and slow
Will lead to the past of no worries we'd all like to think.
And we can carry on all our dreams,
Without worry and gloom by any means.
